MAAT Tranversal

The new programme, called MAAT Transversal, is a (fictitious) monthly programme, to be launched in March 2023 and under the tutelage of Maat, but geographically dispersed throughout the Belém area.

In this context, it was proposed to develop an identity and graphics for the general programme and to design specific pieces for the month of March 2023.

2023, Academic Project
