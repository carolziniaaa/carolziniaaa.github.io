With Love,

The main objective of the ‘With Love,’ project is an emotionally engaging experience, using carefully planned design, which aims to promote reflection, awareness and dialogue on social and ethical issues, the importance of love and empathy in contemporary society.

These letters, coming from a technological future, have as their main objective not only to lead people to reflect and raise awareness about their actions, but also to create a sensory and emotionally impactful experience.

The design thus aims to connect visitors to a “place” where they can explore, feel and interact with the message of love, empathy and social transformation, making the installation ‘With Love,’ a memorable and inspiring experience.

2024, Academic Project
Graphic identity, Editorial, Communication materials, Social media
