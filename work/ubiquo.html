Ubíquo

The project aims to establish a “frontier” between Creativity and Technology, as well as addressing the theme of Artificial Intelligence.

To develop this theme, we designed the graphic identity of the Videocast - Ubíquo* (fictitious). The first season of the videocast consists of four episodes, thus bringing together the entire ETIC community in order to deepen knowledge about the future, whether personally or professionally, considering that artificial intelligence is present in a creative world where there is extreme uncertainty about the future and how we will put this new “tool” into practice.

2023, Academic Project
Graphic identity, Communication materials, Social media
